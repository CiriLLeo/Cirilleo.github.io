Home

Welcome to my humble website. I wanted to open this web page with the image of a tree. The tree, I feel, represents me because at the moment, I am also grounded due to my job. I am alone as I no longer feel a part of the working world that surrounds me, and lastly, I am very resilient because I can carry out my tasks perfectly. However, it is now time to sever these roots and give my all to change my life, finally pursuing something I have always dreamed of becoming a web developer. In the realm of web development, I see an opportunity for growth and transformation. Just as a tree sheds its old leaves to make way for new ones in the spring, I am ready to shed my old life and embrace the new challenges and possibilities that web development has to offer. So, I invite you to join me on this exciting journey through my website. Here, you will witness the transformation of an individual who was once deeply rooted in one world and is now spreading their branches into a new one.
